

### 51. **important** /ɪmˈpɔːtnt/  
**词族**：important (adj.), importance (n.), importantly (adv.)  
**中文**：重要的  
**高频搭配**：  
- **be of (great) importance**：（非常）重要  
  > *Critical thinking is of great importance in academic writing.*  
- **play an important role in**：在……中起重要作用  
  > *Sleep plays an important role in memory consolidation.*

### 52. **different** /ˈdɪfrənt/  
**词族**：different (adj.), differ (v.), difference (n.), differently (adv.)  
**中文**：不同的  
**高频搭配**：  
- **be different from**：与……不同  
  > *Online learning is significantly different from traditional classroom instruction.*  
- **make a difference**：有影响；起作用  
  > *Small changes in teaching methods can make a big difference in student outcomes.*

### 55. **result** /rɪˈzʌlt/

**高频搭配**：
