
### 1. **be** /biː/  
**词族**：be (v.), am, is, are, was, were, being, been  
**中文**：是；存在  
**高频搭配**：  
- **be aware of**：意识到……  
  > *Researchers must be aware of potential biases in their data.*  
- **be responsible for**：对……负责  
  > *The committee is responsible for reviewing all submissions.*

### 2. **have** /hæv/
